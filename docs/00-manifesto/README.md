# 00 · Manifesto

Start here. This is why Tacet exists before it is anything you can click.

Everything else in `docs/` — the product surfaces, the interface guidelines, the
design system, the engineering — is downstream of what's written here. When a
later decision is hard, the answer usually lives in this folder.

## Read in this order

1. **[why-tacet-exists.md](why-tacet-exists.md)** — the problem Tacet answers, and
   the moment it wants you to feel: *why do I have seven apps to talk to the same
   people?*
2. **[founding-principles.md](founding-principles.md)** — the five commitments, in
   full. (The one-page version is [`/FOUNDING_PRINCIPLES.md`](../../FOUNDING_PRINCIPLES.md).)
3. **[anti-patterns.md](anti-patterns.md)** — what Tacet refuses to be. The
   discipline that keeps the principles honest.

## The whole thing in five lines

> People before posts.
> Relationships before engagement.
> Identity before platforms.
> Calm before addiction.
> Open before closed.

## The whole thing in one line

> **Tacet is your home on the open social web.**
