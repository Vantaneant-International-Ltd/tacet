# Compose

Making something and putting it out into the open web. Calmly.

Compose is how you create in Tacet — a post, a photo, a video, a longer piece — and publish it to the open social web. It's a supporting surface: always within reach, never in your face.

## What it is

A single, calm composer for everything you might want to share:

- **Text** — a thought, a note, a reply.
- **Photo** — an image or a set of them.
- **Video** — a clip or a longer piece.
- **Long-form** — writing that wants room to breathe.

Whatever you make here publishes to the open web, reachable by your [People](people.md) and by anyone across the Fediverse who follows you.

## Why it exists

Because a home on the open social web has to let you *speak*, not just listen. Compose is that voice. But it's shaped by [Calm before addiction](../00-manifesto/why-tacet-exists.md): creating should feel like writing a note to people you care about, not like feeding a machine that grades you on performance.

## How it feels

**Calm.** No performance pressure, no "post more to stay relevant," no engagement coaching. You share because you have something to share.

**Non-persistent.** The composer is a place you enter to make a thing and then leave. It doesn't loiter, nag, or sit open baiting you to keep producing. Open it, say your piece, close it. It's reachable from anywhere in Tacet with a single gesture, and gone the moment you're done.

**No vanity scaffolding.** Compose does not dangle predicted reach or optimal posting times or any of the machinery that turns sharing into a numbers game. If drafting exists, it serves you finishing a thought — not a content pipeline.

## Honest about what federates

We are straight with you about where your post goes.

When you publish, it goes to the **open** social web — Tacet and every ActivityPub platform that speaks the same language: Mastodon, Pixelfed, PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica, and others. People on those homes can see and reply to it exactly as if they were on Tacet.

It does **not** go to closed platforms — Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube. Compose will never imply cross-posting to walls that don't open. What federates, federates; what doesn't, doesn't, and we say which is which. See [Discover](discover.md) and the [product vision](product-vision.md).

Different kinds of content live most naturally on different open platforms (video, photos, long-form), and Tacet is honest about how each travels rather than pretending everything renders identically everywhere.

## Reach and audience

Before you publish, you can see and set who a post is for — public to the open web, or narrower. Audience is a clear choice, not a buried setting, and never a growth lever we nudge you to widen. See [Settings](settings.md) for privacy defaults.

The measure for Compose: sharing should feel like reaching your people, not like feeding an algorithm. If it feels like the former, it's working.
