# Discover

Your window into the wider open social web.

Discover is the gateway to the Fediverse — the way you reach everyone out there who isn't already one of your [People](people.md) yet.

## What it is

A calm gateway to the open social web. Through Discover you find new people to follow, explore what's happening across the wider network, and connect to accounts on other platforms — Mastodon, Pixelfed, PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica, and other ActivityPub homes.

It is built from three honest ingredients:

- **A directory.** A browsable, searchable way to find people, communities, and places across the open web.
- **Human recommendation.** Suggestions grounded in real relationships and real curation — who your people follow, who's worth knowing — not in engagement heat.
- **Federation.** The actual reach. Because Tacet speaks ActivityPub, someone on any open platform is reachable and followable from here, with no seams the user has to see.

## Why it exists

[Open before closed](../00-manifesto/why-tacet-exists.md). The whole promise of the open web is that your world isn't trapped inside one company's walls. Discover is where that promise becomes usable — where a normal person, who will never read a word about federated protocols, can nonetheless reach the entire open network.

Federation is infrastructure, not the product. It should feel like email: you don't think about mail servers to write to someone on another provider, and you shouldn't think about instances to follow someone on another platform. See the [product vision](product-vision.md).

## Honest about open vs closed

We tell the truth about reach.

Tacet is the **best home for the open social web.** Through Discover you can genuinely reach Mastodon, Pixelfed, PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica, and other ActivityPub platforms.

Discover does **not** reach closed platforms — Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube. Those are walled by their owners' choice. We will never dangle them as "coming soon" integrations or imply a connection that doesn't exist. If one of them ever opens a real door, we'll walk through it and say so plainly. Until then, Discover shows you the open web, honestly labeled.

## What it is NOT

- **Not an algorithmic trending machine.** No outrage-ranked "what's hot" chasing your attention. No manufactured virality.
- **Not a growth-hacking surface.** Discover helps you find people worth knowing, not maximize impressions.
- **Not a bait for the addictive scroll.** It's a place you go on purpose, find what you came for, and leave.
- **Not a false promise of universal reach.** It connects the open web and says so, no more.

## How it behaves

Discover is intentional. You arrive with a question — who else is out there, what's this community, who does my friend follow — and it answers clearly. Following someone you find here is the same calm gesture as anywhere else; they simply join your [People](people.md), wherever they actually live.

The point of Discover is not to keep you browsing. It's to widen your world and then get you back to the people in it.
