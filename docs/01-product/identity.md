# Identity — "Me"

One identity. You own it. You can leave with it.

"Me" is your identity and your own place on the open social web. It is the surface that makes [Identity before platforms](../00-manifesto/why-tacet-exists.md) real.

## What it is

Your single, owned handle — `@you@tacet.social` — your profile, and your corner of the open web. One name that works everywhere on the open network, the way one email address works across every provider.

This is who you are online. Not a rented account. Not a profile that exists at a company's pleasure. Yours.

## Why it exists

Because on closed platforms, "you" belong to the platform. Your handle, your history, your audience — all held hostage behind a wall you can't take them through. Leave, and you leave everything and everyone behind. That's the leash that keeps people somewhere they no longer want to be.

Tacet cuts the leash. [Identity before platforms](../00-manifesto/why-tacet-exists.md) means the person comes before any product, including ours. "Me" is where that ownership lives.

## What "you own it" means, concretely

Ownership isn't a slogan here. It's a set of guarantees:

- **Portability.** Your identity is not locked to Tacet. You can move to another home on the open web and keep being *you*.
- **You take your people with you.** When you move, your connections move with you. Your [People](people.md) come along; you don't rebuild your world from zero. This is the single most important promise, because it means we have to earn your staying every day.
- **You take your history.** Your posts, your profile, your data — exportable, portable, yours. See [Settings](settings.md) for export and migration.
- **A real door out.** Leaving is a supported, first-class path, not a dark pattern to fight through. If we ever make leaving hard, we've become the thing we're replacing.

An owned identity you can walk away from is the ultimate accountability. It keeps Tacet honest.

## What it is NOT

- **Not a platform-locked account.** Not something you rent and lose on exit.
- **Not tied to Tacet's survival.** If Tacet the company vanished, your identity on the open web should not vanish with it.
- **Not a follower-count trophy.** Your profile is who you are, not a scoreboard. Vanity metrics are retired legacy; see [People](people.md).
- **Not a data harvest dressed as a profile.**

## Your profile and your place

Your profile is a warm, honest representation of a person — not a marketing page optimized for reach. Rich, original, alive; calm, not performative.

Federation stays invisible throughout. You never manage protocols or servers to be yourself here. You have one address, you can live in many homes over time, and the plumbing stays out of sight — exactly like email. See the [product vision](product-vision.md) and [Discover](discover.md).

The test for "Me": could you leave tomorrow, take your identity and your people with you, and still be you? If yes, we've kept the promise.
