# Today

The calm entry point. The first surface you see when you open Tacet.

Today answers one question honestly: **what's worth your attention right now?** Then it lets you go.

## What it is

A bounded digest. A finite, legible set of the things from your people and your world that actually matter today — a few posts worth reading, someone you're close to who shared something, a conversation that's alive, a moment you'd have wanted to know about.

It is a place to land, orient, and leave lighter. Not a place to fall into.

## Why it exists

Every social product starts with an infinite home feed, because an infinite feed maximizes time-on-app. Tacet refuses that trade. But people still need somewhere to land — a first answer to "what should I look at?" Today is that answer, given with respect for the person receiving it.

This is [Calm before addiction](../00-manifesto/why-tacet-exists.md) made concrete. The feed is the window in the home; Today is the doorway you actually walk through.

## What it is NOT

- **Not a "Home Feed."** See the [information architecture](information-architecture.md). The infinite scroll is retired legacy. Today does not pretend to be it under a nicer name.
- **Not infinite.** It has an end. You can reach the bottom, feel finished, and close the app with nothing pulling at you.
- **Not algorithmic compulsion.** Nothing here is tuned to maximize your session length or your return rate. No manufactured "you missed this," no engineered incompleteness.
- **Not a black box.** You can always understand why something is in front of you.

## How it behaves

**Bounded.** Today has a natural shape and a natural end. When you've seen it, you've seen it. "Done" is a real, reachable state — and reaching it should feel good, not like you're being cut off.

**Legible ordering.** Chronological by default, or ordering whose logic is plainly explained. If Today ever groups or prioritizes, it says why in plain language. No opaque ranking chasing outrage or dwell time.

**Quiet by default.** A slow day is a short Today. We never inflate it to keep you busy. An empty-ish Today is a feature: it means your world is calm, and we tell the truth about that.

**Respectful of leaving.** Today is designed for you to finish and go. The best session ends with you closing the app because you're satisfied, not because you tore yourself away.

## How it respects the principles

- **People before posts** — Today leads with your people and what's happening to them, not with whatever content performs.
- **Relationships before engagement** — proximity to your [People](people.md) shapes what surfaces, not raw engagement heat.
- **Calm before addiction** — bounded, legible, finishable. The metric we care about is whether you left settled.

From Today you move naturally into [People](people.md), reply in [Conversations](conversations.md), or step outward into [Discover](discover.md). Today is where the day starts — not where it disappears.
