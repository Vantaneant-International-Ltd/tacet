# Empty states

An empty state is Tacet talking to a person when there's nothing to show. It is one
of the most honest moments in the product, and one of the easiest to ruin. Handle it
the way the rest of Tacet behaves: **state the facts calmly, help without coaxing.**

Empty states are also onboarding-adjacent — the first thing a new person sees in
[Today](navigation.md), [People](navigation.md), or [Conversations](navigation.md)
is often an empty one. First impressions of the *voice* are set here.

## State facts calmly

- **Say what's true, plainly.** "No conversations yet." "You're all caught up."
  "No one here yet." A clear fact, quietly stated, respects the person.
- **"Caught up" is a feature, not a problem.** Because [Today is
  bounded](spacing.md), reaching the end is *success*. Empty and caught-up states
  should feel like calm and completion — the opposite of the infinite feed's "you're
  missing out." Let the person feel finished and free to go.
- **Match the calm of everything else.** No exclamation marks, no urgency, no
  "don't miss out." This is [honesty over manipulation](design-principles.md) at its
  most literal.

## Help without coaching, mascots, or manipulation

- **Offer the next step, once, plainly.** If there is a genuinely useful action —
  find people, start a conversation — offer it as a single clear, honest control.
  One door, not a funnel.
- **No mascots, no cartoons, no cutesy coaching.** No character telling you you're
  doing great, no illustrated hand-holding, no forced cheer. Tacet is a calm home,
  not a gamified app performing enthusiasm at you.
- **No guilt, no FOMO, no fake stakes.** Never imply the person is behind, missing
  out, or letting anyone down by having an empty screen. Manufactured stakes are a
  [dark pattern](../00-manifesto/anti-patterns.md).
- **No dead ends dressed as encouragement.** If help genuinely isn't needed, say the
  simple fact and stop. Silence is allowed; padding is not.

## The honest, quiet voice

The empty-state voice is the whole product's voice, distilled:

- **Plain and warm.** Everyday words, gentle tone. Like a calm friend, not a growth
  team. This is [warmth over austerity](design-principles.md) in copy.
- **Brief.** A short, true sentence beats a paragraph of encouragement. Say it and
  leave room to breathe.
- **Never a sales pitch.** An empty state is not a chance to upsell, cross-promote,
  or juice engagement. It's a quiet, honest moment — treat it as one.

## A quick test

Read the empty state aloud. If it sounds like a friend stating a simple fact and
offering to help *if you want it*, it's right. If it sounds like an app trying to
keep you, rewrite it. The difference between those two sentences is the difference
between a [home and a casino](../00-manifesto/founding-principles.md).
