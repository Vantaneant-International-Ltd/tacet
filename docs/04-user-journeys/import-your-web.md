# Import Your Web

This is where a lesser product would lie to Maya. "Connect your accounts and we'll bring everything over!" — one tap, all seven apps, magic. It would feel wonderful for exactly as long as it took to not work.

Tacet won't do that. Not because it can't dream, but because the whole reason Maya is here is that she's tired of being handled. So this is the honest front door: **Bring your world.**

## What Bring your world really offers

Tacet is the best home for the *open* social web, so this is what it can genuinely do, and it says so in plain terms:

- **Follow your open-web people directly.** If someone lives on Mastodon, Pixelfed, PeerTube, WriteFreely, or Friendica, Maya can find them and follow them right now, and it just works — the way emailing across providers just works (see [first follow](first-follow.md)).
- **Import portable data.** Where a platform gives her a real export — her follow list, her block list, her own posts — Tacet helps her carry it in and settle it into [People](../01-product/people.md). That's data she owns, moved on her terms.
- **Move an existing Fediverse account.** If Maya already has an account on another server, her identity and connections can travel to Tacet properly, the way a home should let you move in with your furniture.

## The honest part

Then Tacet tells her the truth most products hide:

> The closed platforms stay closed. Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn — those are walled gardens, and no login here can unlock them. We won't pretend otherwise.

If she wants people out of those walls, the path is real but human: an export where one exists, a nudge to the friends she cares about to meet her on the open web, patience. Tacet will not fake a bridge to a wall it doesn't control, and it will never sell her a "seamless import" that quietly breaks and leaves her feeling foolish.

## What she feels

Oddly, relief — not disappointment. The honesty lands as trust. A product willing to tell her what it *can't* do is a product she can believe about what it *can*. The seven apps didn't shrink to zero in one tap. But the open half of her world just walked through the door, and the walled half now has a reason — and a place — to follow.

## The principle in play

This is [Open before closed](../00-manifesto/founding-principles.md) and the [anti-patterns](../00-manifesto/anti-patterns.md) refusal to deceive, both at once. "Import your web" means bringing in what open protocols allow and what portable data lets you carry — not a magic key to walled gardens. We'd rather under-promise and be trusted than over-promise and be another disappointment.
