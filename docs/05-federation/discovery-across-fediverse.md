# Discovery Across the Fediverse

Finding your people is the hardest, most human part of any social product. On the open social web, they're scattered across many servers. Tacet's job is to help you find them there — calmly, honestly, and without turning into a trending machine. This is the federated face of **[Discover](../01-product/discover.md)**.

## What we're trying to solve

You want to find the people and communities worth your attention, wherever they live. `@anna@mastodon.social`, a photography community on Pixelfed, a writer on WriteFreely. The open web makes this possible but not easy, because there's no single index and no owner. Tacet leans into three honest sources of discovery rather than one addictive one.

## Three honest sources

**1. Directory.** A searchable, well-kept way to find accounts and communities by handle, name, or interest across the open web. Like looking someone up — deliberate, on your terms. You search; you decide.

**2. Human recommendation.** People before posts means the best signal is often a person you trust saying "follow this one." Tacet favours recommendations that come from real people and editorial care over machine-ranked virality.

**3. Federation itself.** The web of follows *is* a discovery surface. Who the people you already trust follow and talk to reveals more good people — surfaced gently, never as an infinite pull.

## What this is *not*

Explicitly, so no one mistakes the intent:

- **Not an algorithmic trending engine.** No manufactured "what's blowing up," no engagement leaderboard, no outrage amplifier. That's Relationships before engagement and Calm before addiction, applied to discovery.
- **Not a growth-hacking funnel.** We help you find people you'll actually want in your life, not the maximum number of accounts to inflate a count.
- **Not a claim to index everything.** The open web is large and we can only faithfully cover the ActivityPub-compatible platforms — Mastodon, Pixelfed, PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica and their kin. We don't reach into closed platforms, and we say so — see [federation principles](federation-principles.md).

## Honest by default

Discovery on Tacet stays calm on purpose. Results are things you sought or people you trust vouched for, not a slot machine dressed as a feed. When we can't find or can't reach something, we say so plainly. The point of discovery is to end in a real relationship — a follow you're glad you made — not in more time spent scrolling.
