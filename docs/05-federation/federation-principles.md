# Federation Principles

How Tacet federates, stated as principles rather than plumbing. These sit directly under the five: People before posts, Relationships before engagement, **Identity before platforms, Calm before addiction, Open before closed.** Federation is where those last three become real.

## 1. The protocol is invisible

The user never meets ActivityPub. No "instances," no "fediverse," no "toots" or "boosts" as vocabulary they must learn. They have a handle — `@you@tacet.social` — and people they follow. Some of those people happen to live on other servers. That fact should surface only when it's genuinely useful (like a source label on a post), never as a tax on attention. If a design decision forces a person to think about the protocol, the design is wrong. See [ActivityPub as infrastructure](activitypub-as-infrastructure.md).

## 2. Honesty over hype

Tacet does not oversell what federation can do. We say plainly what works, what's partial, and what isn't possible. The line we hold:

> **"Tacet is the best home for the open social web."**
> Not: "Tacet connects to every closed social network."

Open, ActivityPub-compatible platforms — Mastodon, Pixelfed, PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica, and others that speak the protocol — are home turf. Closed platforms (Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube) stay walled gardens until they open a real, official door. We will not fake a bridge that isn't there, and we'll tell you when something is a walled garden rather than pretending otherwise.

## 3. Calm across servers

The wider open web is large and noisy. Tacet's job is to make it feel calm anyway — the same calm we owe on our own server. Remote posts arrive into the same unhurried surfaces, with no engagement traps smuggled in from elsewhere. Federation must never become a back door for addiction mechanics. Calm before addiction applies to every server, not just ours.

## 4. Portability is a promise, not a feature

Because the protocol is open, your identity is yours to move. You can leave Tacet and take your people with you; you can arrive from elsewhere and keep your world. This is the ultimate expression of Open before closed, and it's covered fully in [portability](portability.md).

## What Tacet will and won't claim

| We will say | We won't say |
| --- | --- |
| "Follow anyone on the open social web." | "Follow anyone, anywhere, on any app." |
| "Faithfully shows posts from Mastodon, Pixelfed, and more." | "Full Instagram / X / TikTok integration." |
| "Your identity is portable — take your people with you." | "You'll never need another account again." |
| "Some closed platforms are walled gardens; we're honest about that." | "Tacet connects to every network." |

Honesty is not a limitation to apologise for. It's the reason people can trust the calm.
