# Brand

**Your home on the open social web.**

Tacet is one identity you own, one place your people live, and one calm window
into the wider Fediverse. Not another social network chasing attention — a home
you keep.

## The name

**Tacet** is a musical direction: an instruction for an instrument to fall
silent. It names the thing we protect — quiet, attention, the space to actually
be with your people. See [naming](naming.md).

The domain is `tacet.social`. Your address looks like `@you@tacet.social`.
Tacet is a venture of the VNTA Group.

## Positioning in brief

We lead with what Tacet **is**, never with what it removes.

- Your people, in one place, instead of seven apps for the same faces.
- One identity you own, that travels the open web like an email address.
- A calm home — the absence of ads and manipulation is fine print, not the pitch.

Tacet is the best home for the **open** social web: Mastodon, Pixelfed,
PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica. We are honest about that boundary. We do not
claim to connect closed platforms. See [launch positioning](launch-positioning.md).
