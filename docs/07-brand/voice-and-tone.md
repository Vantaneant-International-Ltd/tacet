# Voice and tone

Tacet sounds like a person you trust telling you something true, plainly.

## The voice

Five words hold it: **calm, warm, honest, spare, confident.**

- **Calm.** No urgency theater, no exclamation marks doing emotional work. We are the rest in the bar, not the drum roll.
- **Warm.** Human and familiar, not clinical, not austere. We are talking to a person about their people.
- **Honest.** We never overstate, never imply what isn't true. Honesty is the brand, not a compliance step.
- **Spare.** Short lines. One idea per sentence. We cut before we add.
- **Confident.** Quiet is a choice, so we state things plainly and don't hedge. No apologizing for restraint.

The reference point is an Apple keynote at its clearest: precise, unhurried,
sure of itself. Not SaaS hype, not corporate, not academic, not cutesy.

## What we sound like

> Your people, in one place.

> One address. Every home on the open web.

> Reply to a friend on Mastodon the same way you'd reply to a friend here.

> This is your feed, in the order things happened. Nothing reordered it.

## What we never sound like

- **Hype:** "The revolutionary social platform that changes everything!" — no.
- **Growth-hacky:** "Don't miss out — invite 5 friends to unlock..." — never.
- **Corporate:** "Tacet leverages federated protocols to enable..." — no jargon.
- **Cutesy:** "Oopsie! Something went wonky 🙃" — warm is not childish.
- **Negative-first:** "No ads. No algorithm. No tracking." — true, but it's fine print, not the pitch. Lead with the home, not the list of things we removed.

## The honesty rule (non-negotiable)

Tacet is the best home for the **open** social web — Mastodon, Pixelfed,
PeerTube, WriteFreely, Friendica.

- ✅ "Tacet is the best home for the open social web."
- ✅ "Follow and reply to people across the Fediverse."
- ❌ "Tacet connects to every network you use."
- ❌ "Bring your Instagram / X / TikTok / LinkedIn / YouTube into Tacet."

Closed platforms appear in our copy for exactly one reason: to name the
fragmentation problem — *seven apps for the same people.* We never imply
integration with them. If we cannot do it, we do not say it. See
[launch positioning](launch-positioning.md).
