# Future

Past [v1](v1.md): the work that makes Tacet not just *a* home on the open social
web, but **the best** one. Directional, not dated — and deliberately not
overpromised. We name where we're heading, and we don't dress ambition up as
availability.

## Deeper federation

The open web is large and still growing. Over time Tacet should feel native
across more of it — more content types, more graceful handling of remote quirks,
more of the [Fediverse](../05-federation/README.md) reachable from one calm home.
We deepen support for the open protocols (ActivityPub and its neighbors) as they
mature. See [activitypub-as-infrastructure](../05-federation/activitypub-as-infrastructure.md).

We do **not** promise integration with closed platforms — Instagram, X, TikTok,
LinkedIn, YouTube. That boundary is permanent, not a milestone we're saving for
later. See [launch positioning](../07-brand/launch-positioning.md).

## Portability you can feel

Identity before platforms means you can leave. The future goal is portability
that isn't just technically true but *felt*: move your address, your people, and
your history to another home without losing yourself, the way you'd move email.
The protocol makes it possible; our job is to make it painless. See
[portability](../05-federation/portability.md).

A home you can't leave isn't a home. So we build the door, and we make it good.

## Mobile

Tacet where your people actually are — in your pocket, calm. A mobile experience
that keeps the same quiet: no engagement notifications engineered to pull you
back, no infinite reasons to reopen. Presence when you want it, silence when you
don't. Faithful to [calm before addiction](../00-manifesto/founding-principles.md).

## Calm defaults, everywhere

The long game is defaults. The best home for the open social web is the one whose
out-of-the-box settings already protect you — chronological by default, quiet
notifications by default, no dark patterns anywhere, ever. See
[anti-patterns](../00-manifesto/anti-patterns.md).

Every future feature earns its place against one test: does it make Tacet calmer
and more yours, or busier and more theirs? Only the former ships.

## The honest note

This page is a direction, not a delivery schedule. We'd rather under-claim and
build the real thing than list a roadmap of promises. When something here is
built, the [brand](../07-brand/README.md) will say so plainly — and not a
sentence sooner.
