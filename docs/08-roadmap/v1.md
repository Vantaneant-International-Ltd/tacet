# v1

Where Tacet becomes a home a person genuinely **prefers** over the seven apps it
replaces. The [MVP](mvp.md) proves the promise; v1 makes it a place you'd choose.

Deeper, but still principled. Every addition here is measured against the same
question: does this serve your people, or does it serve engagement? If the
latter, it doesn't ship. See [founding principles](../00-manifesto/founding-principles.md).

## What v1 adds

### Full Compose

Posting as a first-class, calm act — text and media, drafts, replies, and
sharing across the open web. Composed for expression, not for reach-chasing;
no "boost your post" nudges, no optimal-time coaching. See [compose](../01-product/compose.md).

### Communities

Shared spaces done right — the reshaped, principled version of what the old
"rooms/clubhouse" app gestured at, now serving relationships rather than
walled-garden lock-in. Open where it can be, calm by default. See
[communities](../01-product/communities.md).

### Conversations

Replies and threads that read like people talking, not comment sections
optimized for volume. The place a real back-and-forth lives, gathered and
legible, across servers. See [conversations](../01-product/conversations.md).

### Discover

Finding people and worth-your-time content **without** an attention algorithm.
Human, browsable, honest discovery across the open web — you looking, not a
system deciding for you. See [discover](../01-product/discover.md).

### A polished, calm home

The whole experience tightened: the [design system](../03-design-system) applied
consistently, the [human interface guidelines](../02-human-interface-guidelines)
honored, the [user journeys](../04-user-journeys) smooth end to end. Fast, quiet,
sure of itself.

## Federation at v1

Broader than the MVP's read/follow/reply, still honest. Composing outward,
richer remote profiles, cross-server conversations that hold together, and
discovery that reaches across the [Fediverse](../05-federation/README.md). We
expand the surface only as far as we can make it genuinely work.

Still no closed-platform integration — that boundary never moves. See
[launch positioning](../07-brand/launch-positioning.md).

## The v1 bar

A person opens Tacet in the morning instead of opening five other apps, and it
feels lighter than what it replaced. Their people are here. Their identity is
theirs. Nothing is trying to keep them scrolling. That is v1: not more features
than the incumbents, but the **right** ones, calmly.

Beyond this, see [future](future.md).
