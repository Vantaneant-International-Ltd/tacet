The feed post. Author row + body + optional media inset + quiet action row (heart, reply, share, bookmark).

```jsx
<PostCard author="Maya Chen" handle="@maya@tacet.home" time="2h" likes={24} replies={6}>
  Just finished a new illustration series about small, hopeful internet moments.
</PostCard>
```

Counts are understated; `liked` fills the heart in rose.
